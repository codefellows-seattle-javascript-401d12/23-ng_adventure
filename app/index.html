Welcome to ngMarsMission

Ooops. Turns out climate change was real, and Earth is no longer inhabitable.

Luckily, you know the right people and secured a ticket to Mars to continue your survival.

You need to find your way to the Utopia Planitia Mars station.

You can survive there for about 2 years with the given supplies.

You'll have to figure out how to survive after that on your own. Best of luck!
